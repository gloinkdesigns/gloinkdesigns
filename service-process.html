Process

  1. Contact me
    DM on instagram or send an email at athirajayachandran111@gmail.com with all your requirements. And I will book a slot for you.
  2. Reference & Details
    You can send me the details as text or as any separate reference photos for the portrait / character face, hairstyle, outfit, pose, pets, background or any other elements to add (eg. books, coffee, headhone, accessories, chair, plants, shelf decor etc.)
  3. Rough Sketch
    I will send a rough sketch of your artwork on the booked date. (If its a character / book cover artwork, then will send a mood board also, if needed)
  4. Payment
    Once the sketch is finalized, payment can be made through my Paypal link.
  5. Final Delivery
    On completion of the design, Final files will be send to yout email. Final deliverable include JPEG files for personal artworks. JPEG, PNG and PDF files of required size for commercial artworks.
